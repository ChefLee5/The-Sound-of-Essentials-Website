📅 How Each Day Works

Every daily page has 6 Activity Blocks:

  • 📖 Block A — Reading & Language (Harmonia, 3 min)
  • 🔢 Block B — Math (Numeria, 3 min)
  • 🌿 Block C — Science/Nature (Terrasol, 3 min)
  • 💪 Block D — Fitness Move (Vitalis, 2 min)
  • 🌍 Block E — Rotating Subject (M/W = Geography · T/Th = Civics · F = Review, 2 min)
  • ✨ Block F — Daily Reflection (Celestia, 2 min)

Total time: approximately 15 minutes. Check each box when done ✅

⭐ Tips for Summer Stretch Success

  • ✅ Do your workbook at the same time every day to build a habit
  • ✅ Find a quiet, well-lit spot with a pencil and something to drink nearby
  • ✅ It's okay to skip a day — just do two short sessions the next day
  • ✅ Look up words you don't know! Curiosity builds vocabulary
  • ✅ Share what you're learning with a family member at dinner
  • ✅ Celebrate every Friday — you completed another week!
  • ✅ Write neatly. Your future self will thank you
🌐

Bilingual Learners: You will notice IPA phonetic guides throughout this workbook. These show you how to pronounce English words. Whenever you see a phonetic guide, try to say the word aloud. Then write it in your home language in the space provided!

Aprendices bilingues: El IPA le ayuda con la pronunciación. ¡Escriba también en su idioma nativo!
